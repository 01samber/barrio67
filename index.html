About Us

Barrio 67 is a bohemian resto-pub nestled in the heart of Batroun's Old Souk. Blending vintage charm with seasonal flavors, handcrafted cocktails, and live music, it offers a vibrant yet intimate atmosphere every night of the week.

  • Seasonal menu crafted from fresh local ingredients
  • Expertly mixed cocktails at our full-service bar
  • Live music performances throughout the week
  • Open daily — your escape awaits
